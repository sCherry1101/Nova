Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nova?

Nova is a futuristic productivity and anti-procrastination platform designed to help you level up your daily tasks and achieve your goals. It incorporates gamified elements and intuitive tools to keep you focused and motivated.

How does the timer work?

The timer is a core feature of Nova, allowing you to set focused work intervals. You can start, pause, and reset it. It's designed to help you practice time management techniques like the Pomodoro Technique, breaking down your work into manageable chunks.

What are "Quests"?

Quests are your personalized to-do list items. Instead of just listing tasks, Nova frames them as "quests" to make your productivity journey feel more engaging and adventurous. Completing quests helps you progress within the Nova system.

Can I customize my profile?

Yes, your profile page allows you to see your selected codename, the date you joined, and your current status. Future updates may include more customization options to personalize your Nova experience.

Is Nova available on mobile devices?

Nova is designed with responsiveness in mind, meaning it should adapt well to various screen sizes, including mobile phones and tablets. For the best experience, we recommend using a modern web browser.

How do I change my codename?

Currently, your codename is set upon your first login. To change it, you would typically need to clear your browser's local storage for this site or use a dedicated profile settings feature, which might be introduced in future updates.

What if I forget to stop the timer?

The timer will automatically stop when it reaches zero. While it doesn't automatically pause or reset, you can manually stop it at any time using the "Stop Timer" button if you need a break or finish early.

How can I provide feedback or suggest features?

We highly value user feedback! While there isn't a direct feedback form on the current version, you can imagine a future "Contact Us" or "Feedback" section where you could submit your ideas and bug reports. Your input helps us make Nova better!

Are there any premium features?

In this current iteration, all features are available to everyone. Future versions might explore premium tiers with advanced analytics, more customization, or exclusive quest types, but the core anti-procrastination tools will always be accessible.

What is the purpose of the "About Us" page?

The "About Us" page provides information about the vision behind Nova, its mission to combat procrastination, and the principles guiding its development. It's where you can learn more about the philosophy driving your productivity journey.
